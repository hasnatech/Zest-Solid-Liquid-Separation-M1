Working principle of decanter centrifuge
  • The decanter centrifuge uses centrifugal forces that can be well beyond 3000 times greater than gravity
  • Solid particles are pressed outwards against the rotating bowl wall, while the less dense liquid phase forms a concentric inner layer
  • The sediment formed by the solids is continuously removed by the screw conveyor, which rotates at a different speed than the bowl
  • The solids are gradually “ploughed” out and removed from the conical part as a result
